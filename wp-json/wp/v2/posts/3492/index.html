Who exactly will be speaking?<\/p>\n

    \n
  • James Altucher<\/strong> \u2013 A hedge fund manager, entrepreneur, best-selling author, venture capitalist, podcaster, and Bitcoin believer. He\u2019s founded 20 companies including StockPickr, and he frequently contributes to TechCrunch, TheStreet, and the Financial Times. Now he\u2019s got his finger on Bitcoin\u2019s pulse, and you don\u2019t want to miss what he\u2019s going to reveal.<\/li>\n
  • Tim Draper<\/strong> \u2013 Tim is a venture capitalist, and his firm was one of the early investors in Skype, has his fingers in a lot of pies, including crypto and blockchain ventures. He’s going to show you where the smart money is.<\/li>\n
  • John McAfee<\/strong> \u2013 Yes, that McAfee. He\u2019s the guy who created McAfee internet security. He also famously said he\u2019d eat his you-know-what on live TV if Bitcoin didn\u2019t reach $500k in three years. John\u2019s going to show you why he\u2019s not nervous about that at all.<\/li>\n
  • Ronnie Moas<\/strong> \u2013 Ronnie was a Wall Street money man, and he founded Standpoint Research. He and his team deliver stock analysis daily. When bitcoin was still a joke to most, Ronnie predicted it would hit $11k. Then $14k. Then $20k. Wait until you hear his next predictions.<\/li>\n
  • Erik Voorhees<\/strong> \u2013 Erik co-founded the bitcoin company Coinapult and is also the creator and CEO of ShapeShift.io, the instant bitcoin and altcoin exchange. He\u2019s been a featured guest on Bloomberg, Fox Business, CNBC, BBC Radio, The Peter Schiff Show, and numerous industry conferences. Erik is no fan of the Feds or taxes and keeps his finances and assets in bitcoin.<\/li>\n
  • Charlie Shrem<\/strong> \u2013 Charlie has been featured in two Bitcoin documentaries: \u201cThe Rise and Rise of Bitcoin,\u201d and \u201cBanking on Bitcoin\u201d and he has a lot to say about where the blockchain revolution is headed. Charlie is also the founder of the Bitcoin Foundation (Vice-Chairman 2012 to 2014) and worked on the standardization, protection, and promotion of Bitcoin.<\/li>\n
  • Lyn Ulbricht<\/strong> \u2013 Lyn is the mother of Ross Ulbricht. Ross was handed a double life sentence for creating the Silk Road marketplace. The problem is, they were all non-violent charges, and no victims came forward in any way. By contrast, Noriega got 25 years. Having seen her only son incarcerated for life after a trial rife with proven corruption, Lyn is dedicated to fighting not only for Ross\u2019s freedom but for the thousands who have been victimized by our flawed criminal justice system and the drug war. She\u2019ll be talking about Digital Privacy.<\/li>\n

    This is what \u201cSoft Fork\u201d attendees will get:<\/p>\n

    • Live stream of the main conference<\/strong> \u2013 The second venue will be hooked up with state-of-the-art AV equipment, so attendees can take part in all of the keynote and other \u201cmain conference\u201d talks while being able to interact with the speakers and ask questions.<\/li>\n
    • Exclusive live (i.e. in-person) talks<\/strong> \u2013 Some of the keynote speakers have agreed to being shuttled across to the second venue, so that they can deliver a second<\/em> exclusive talk to the \u201cSoft Fork\u201d attendees. Additionally, there will be some speakers who are only<\/em> speaking at the second \u201cSoft Fork\u201d venue. (Details on this will be announced shortly \u2013 so subscribe to the Super Conference notification list<\/strong><\/a>.)<\/strong><\/li>\n
    • Complimentary audio and video recordings ($297 value)<\/strong> \u2013 \u201cSoft Fork\u201d attendees will receive a complimentary DVD\/CD boxset after the event containing full audio and video recordings of every talk at both events, as well as the optional beginners educational track. (This special gift is reserved for \u201cSoft Fork\u201d ticket holders only. Regular tickets holders will need to pay for these recordings.)<\/li>\n
    • More surprises (details to be announced)<\/strong> \u2013 Mr. Jacobs is putting together a few surprise bonuses for everyone who registers as a \u201cSoft Fork\u201d attendee.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
