Execution is a rare quality in people. \u00a0I have found that rarely ever do people know how to actually execute an idea. \u00a0If you take something that is rare amongst people it becomes even harder to find when we put together a group of people. \u00a0I think this is where we have gotten to when it comes to the core developers. \u00a0I think that they are just afraid to act on a scaling plan for bitcoin. \u00a0It seems to me that they are so worried about bitcoin failing if they make a move that they are crippling bitcoin to stagnation. \u00a0Bitcoin is the honey badger of money, and that honey badger don\u2019t give a f***!<\/p>\n
