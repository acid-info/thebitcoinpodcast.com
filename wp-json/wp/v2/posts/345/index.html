One word, Lawnmower<\/a>. One site, Lawnmower.io<\/a>. One app, Lawnmower<\/a>. So much beauty in simplicity.<\/p>\n

So, how does this company and their app make your life easy? \u00a0They take the thought out of saving bitcoin, and the stress too. \u00a0Similar to any standard issue \u201ckeep the change\u201d promotion at your local money printing shop, Lawnmower will take the leftover change from your purchases\u00a0needed to make a dollar and stack it up for you. \u00a0However, instead of stacking it up in a savings account that is going to end as a loss of wealth over time with that oh so hot 1% return on your savings, Lawnmower takes this spare change and buys bitcoin with it. \u00a0With seamless, smooth,\u00a0and appealing UI and an amazing worry free feel, you can save up the primo digital crypto all while simply just living your life. \u00a0Swipe the card for a dinner date \u00ad\u00adsecuring your future! Because, you secured me twenty\u00ad-five years ago!\u201d \u00a0Although that last hypothetical situation is highly unlikely I do believe\u00a0you understand the amazingness of the Lawnmower app. \u00a0With nifty features like multipliers that stack up your change even quicker, and purchase thresholds to save you money on fees,<\/p>\n
